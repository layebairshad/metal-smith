About Us

At Metal Smith, we are passionate about creating lighting and decor that seamlessly blends sustainability with elegance. Established in 2009, we specialize in manufacturing and exporting premium lighting and handcrafted decor made from brass, aluminum, and stainless steel. Based in Moradabad, Uttar Pradesh, we take pride in our state-of-the-art infrastructure and skilled artisans who create exquisite designs with flawless finishes. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us a loyal clientele across the globe. With a focus on innovative craftsmanship, we aim to deliver timeless decorative pieces that blend tradition with modernity.
